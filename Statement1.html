When we were given the theme "unseen" the first thing that came to my mind was invisible, something no one can see just like our thoughts. Through this assignment I tried to represent a part of my thought process.

Since we cannot see what a person is thinking we need to communicate to understand what is going on their mind. On my second page SIT.I showed someone asking what is on their mind. When you proceed into the thought bubble which seems to be blank, leads to bubbleMind. That page is a representation of my mind. A busy mind, a mind that wonders a lot. And it gets hard at times to be present. I relate my thoughts as bubbles. When there’s many bubbles and you try to touch one, it disappears, just like my thoughts. When ever I try to engage with my thoughts, I jump to another one and most of the time can not remember what I was even thinking. So, when I am asked what is on my mind it’s hard to explain so I have put a tear drop on that page that leads to negative self talk. Most of the harm that is caused in our head, is by us and our thoughts. On THOUGHTS I tried to show self-critical version of my mind. I can be thinking everything and nothing at the same time, which is often overwhelming. I was once told that if I want to feed my problem I can keep thinking about it but if I want to starve the problem I need to take action.WALK is the first action that I take to get away from my own overwhelming thoughts. Thoughts that are unseen but can be disturbing at times. I go for a walk for fresh air and try to remind myself that it’s okay to feel that way. On that page there’s heart on one of the building, that is my happy place. It is the place with the people who are patient with me and tries to understand the unseen with me.
